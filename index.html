Privacy Policy for SCA Marriage

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Welcome to SCA Marriage. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our matchmaking services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your Name, Phone Number, and Email Address.
  • Demographic & Profile Data: Date of Birth, Gender, Religion, Caste/Kulam, Education, and Occupation.
  • Financial & Preference Data: Your Monthly Income and partner income preferences.
  • Location Data: Approximate location data such as your District, State, and Taluk.
  • Verification Documents: Profile photos, House images, Horoscope images, and ID documents (such as Aadhaar) to verify your identity.
  • App Activity & Messaging: Your interactions within the app (e.g., likes, profile views, search history) and in-app chat messages with your matches.
  • Device Information: Your Device FCM Token to send you push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, and your consent. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Facilitate Matchmaking: To display your profile to potential matches and personalize your daily recommendations.
  • Account Creation & Management: To verify your phone number via OTP and manage your account.
  • Fraud Prevention: To verify your identity using the uploaded Aadhaar and photo documents, ensuring a safe community.
  • Communication: To send you push notifications regarding matches, messages, or account updates.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your profile information (such as Name, photos, location, and demographic details) is shared only with other registered users of the SCA Marriage platform for the purpose of matchmaking.

We may share your device token data with third-party service providers (such as Google Firebase) solely for the purpose of delivering push notifications and maintaining app stability.

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption to protect your data in transit between your mobile device and our servers. While we implement standard security practices to protect your information, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless otherwise required by law.

Your Right to Delete Data:
You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the SCA Marriage app by navigating to the Settings/Profile section and selecting the Delete Account option.

Once you request account deletion, your profile, photos, and personal information will be permanently removed from our active databases.

6. Access Restrictions for Minors

SCA Marriage is a matrimony service intended solely for adults seeking marriage. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.
