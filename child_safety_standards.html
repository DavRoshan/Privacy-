Child Safety Standards & Protection Policy

App Name: Golla Arunthathiyar Matrimony (SCA Marriage)

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

At Golla Arunthathiyar Matrimony, we are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and secure environment for our community. As a platform dedicated to matrimony and serious relationships, we maintain the highest standards when it comes to the safety and protection of minors.

1. Zero Tolerance for Child Exploitation

Golla Arunthathiyar Matrimony has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), child sexual abuse, and any form of child exploitation or endangerment (CSAE). We strictly prohibit any content, messaging, or profile that promotes, facilitates, or depicts the exploitation of children.

2. Age Requirements & Verification

  • Adults Only: Our services are strictly for individuals aged 18 and older. Any user found to be a minor will have their account terminated immediately.
  • Strict Verification: We implement multi-layered verification processes, including Aadhaar card verification and profile photo approval, to ensure that every participant in our community is an adult and who they claim to be.

3. Prohibited Content & Behaviors

The following are strictly prohibited on our platform:

  • Uploading or sharing images or videos depicting minors.
  • Any communication that targets or exploits children.
  • Using the platform for illegal activities involving minors.

4. In-App Reporting & Enforcement

We empower our users to maintain a safe community through robust reporting tools:

  • Report Feature: Every profile and conversation includes a "Report" button. We encourage users to report any suspicious activity or content that violates our safety standards.
  • Rapid Response: Our safety team prioritizes reports related to minor safety and child exploitation. Content that violates this policy is removed immediately, and offending accounts are permanently banned.

5. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

In accordance with global and regional child safety laws, we cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies. We will report any instances of potential child sexual abuse or exploitation identified on our platform to the relevant national authorities and reporting centers (such as NCMEC or regional equivalents).
